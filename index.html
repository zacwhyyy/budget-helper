How much should I spend?

Have you ever wondered how much you should spend each month?
Enter your monthly income below and we will calculate it for you.

Expenses

Set aside this amount of your income for the essentials such as groceries, utilities, gas for your car and rent. It is important to understand what is considered a "need" vs. a "want"!

-
"Wants"

Spend on things that you want but could live without. This $$ allows for flexible spending and, perhaps, a happier life. But we would suggest you to invest in yourself though, buy some books, join some courses.

-
Personal Savings

This is for your savings and debt repayments. In other words, paying off the past and investing in the future.
